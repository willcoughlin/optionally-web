How OptionAlly helps you evaluate options trades

1. Select underlying asset

Choose from any stock or ETF traded on a major US exchange.

2. Choose a strategy

OptionAlly has strategies that cater to your goals and skill level. For the bulls, the bears, and those in between.

3. Configure trade

Pick option contracts to configure the legs of the trade.

4. Evaluate results

Use the calculated risk/reward ratio and hypothetical profit or loss scenarios to judge the strategy and avoid outsized risk.
